About Us

About Us

Welcome to Drosh Inn, your serene escape in the heart of nature! Nestled away from the hustle and bustle of the Drosh bazaar, we offer a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking comfort and relaxation. Our guest house is designed to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making you feel at home from the moment you arrive. .

Our History

Drosh Inn has its roots in the vibrant Drosh main bazaar, where we began our journey as City View Hotel in 2015. With a vision to create a peaceful haven, we decided to open Drosh Inn, allowing guests to enjoy the beauty of the region while being distanced from the lively market scene. Our commitment to exceptional hospitality remains the cornerstone of our establishment.

What We Do

At Drosh Inn, we strive to provide an unforgettable experience for our guests. Our services include:
- Comfortable Accommodations: We offer a range of cozy rooms equipped with modern amenities to ensure a restful stay.
- Local Cuisine: Enjoy delicious meals made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients, showcasing the flavors of the region.
- Personalized Service: Our friendly staff is dedicated to making your stay enjoyable, providing tailored recommendations and assistance.
- Activities and Tours: Explore the breathtaking landscapes and cultural heritage of Drosh through guided tours and outdoor activities. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or an extended stay, Drosh Inn is committed to making your visit memorable..
